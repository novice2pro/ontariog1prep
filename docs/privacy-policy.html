NSW: DKT Simulator & Study Guide does not collect, process, or store any personal data from users. The app is designed solely for educational purposes and operates entirely without accessing or processing user-specific information.

Source of Information

Information provided in the app is based on the official NSW Road User Handbook, accessible at the New South Wales government website: NSW Road User Handbook.

When using the app, non-personally identifiable log data may be collected in case of errors. This data may include device information such as IP address, operating system version, and time and date of use, but it is not linked to any individual and is used solely for app improvement.

NSW: DKT Simulator & Study Guide takes reasonable measures to protect data transmitted over the internet. However, it notes that no transmission over a public network is entirely secure.

The app may contain links to external sites not operated by NSW: DKT Simulator & Study Guide. Users are advised to review the privacy policies of these sites and services, as the app assumes no responsibility for their content, policies, or practices.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is inadvertently collected, it will be deleted immediately.
